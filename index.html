Order Guidelines

  • You may mix and match different items to qualify for the volume pricing tiers (10 plus or 50 plus total cases).
  • Cases themselves cannot be mixed. Each case must contain a single SKU, but any combination of full cases may be used to reach a volume tier.
  • Orders of 10 or more total cases qualify for the Closeout 10 plus price level.
  • Orders of 50 or more total cases qualify for the Closeout 50 plus price level.
  • Any combination of SKUs across the catalog may be used to reach 10 or 50 total cases.
  • Prices are FOB our Los Angeles warehouse. Shipping is not included.
  • We can provide a freight quote upon request, or you may schedule your own carrier pickup.
  • All closeout merchandise is final sale, and all items are sold on a first come first served basis while supplies last.
